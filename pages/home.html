About Tiny Tails

We're a family-run pet care service dedicated to providing your furry friends with the love and attention they deserve while you're away.

Our eco-friendly approach means we reuse packaging, choose sustainable products, and minimise our environmental pawprint.

Fully licensed and insured, with a safe, cosy environment where your pets will feel comfortable, safe, and happy.
