Hi, I'm Aleshia

I started Tiny Tails because I believe pets deserve more than cold kennels when their owners are away. Having grown up with dogs all my life, I understand how important it is to maintain their routine and provide genuine care.

When your pet stays with me, they become part of my family. We'll go for walks in the local countryside, snuggle on the sofa, and follow their normal routine as much as possible. I only take a small number of pets at a time to ensure everyone gets the attention they deserve.

Why choose Tiny Tails?

  • Fully insured and DBS-checked for your peace of mind
  • No kennels - your pet stays in my home as part of the family
  • Never left alone for long periods
  • Daily updates with photos so you know they're happy
